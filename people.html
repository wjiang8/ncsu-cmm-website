Dr. Wen Jiang

Assistant Professor, Nuclear Engineering · Joint Appointment, Idaho National Laboratory

Dr. Jiang develops numerical methods for multiphysics and multiscale modeling of nuclear fuel and structural materials, with emphasis on interfaces and damage. He received his Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science from Duke University, and spent several years at Idaho National Laboratory contributing to the BISON fuel performance code before joining NC State.
