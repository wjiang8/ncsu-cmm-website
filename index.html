Research thrusts

all projects →
phase-field

Fracture of Nuclear Fuels

Variational phase-field methods for crack nucleation and growth in UO₂ and coated-particle fuels, coupled to heat transport and irradiation physics.

triso / fcm

Coated Particle Fuel Performance

Statistical failure analysis and thermomechanics of TRISO and fully ceramic microencapsulated fuel for microreactors and HTGRs.

ml-surrogates

AI-Accelerated Simulation

Machine-learning surrogates, active learning, and multifidelity methods for fission gas release and rapid fuel qualification.

methods

Multiphysics Method Development

Interface-enriched finite elements, cohesive zone models, and contact algorithms contributed to the MOOSE ecosystem.
